Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does customization work?
After subscribing, you'll complete a wellness profile telling us your preferences, goals, and any allergies. Our algorithm and wellness experts use this to personalize each box. You can update your preferences anytime.
Can I skip a month?
Absolutely! You can skip any month with no penalty. Just log into your account and pause your subscription before the billing date. Your next box will ship the following month.
What's your cancellation policy?
You can cancel anytime with just a few clicks. We don't believe in lock-in contracts. If you cancel mid-cycle, you'll receive any remaining boxes you've paid for.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently we ship to the US, Canada, UK, and EU countries. International shipping fees may apply outside the US. We're working to expand to more countries soon!
Are the products full-size or samples?
Most items are full-size, though we occasionally include deluxe samples of premium products. Our Balance and Premium plans guarantee all full-size products.
How do refills work?
Found a product you love? Log into your account to order refills at a subscriber discount. We'll even remind you when it's time to reorder your favorites.

Still have questions? Contact our support team
